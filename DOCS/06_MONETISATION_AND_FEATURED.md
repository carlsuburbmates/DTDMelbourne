# 06 — Monetisation and Featured Placement (FINAL, LOCKED)

This document is subordinate to **SSOT_FINAL.md** and exists to provide a single, clear reference for **pricing, GST, refunds, ordering, and featured capacity**.

## 1) Pricing (AUD)

- **Pro (Gold Card):** **$20 AUD** per **30 days**, **auto-renewing** subscription.
- **Featured placement (add-on):** **$15 AUD** per **30 days**, **non-recurring**, manual re-purchase.
- **Basic:** **Free** ($0).

### GST

- All advertised prices are in **AUD** and are **GST-inclusive (where GST applies)**.

## 2) Products

### Basic (Free)

- Visibility: appears in directory and council results.
- Profile: standard.
- Badge: none.
- Ordering: lowest priority (see ordering rules).

### Pro — Gold Card

- Cost: **$20 AUD / 30 days** (auto-renew).
- Badge: **Gold Card**.
- Visibility: appears in directory and council results.
- Priority: **top suggested first within council**, below Featured.
- Pro-only feature: **Landing Page Builder** (built-in page they can build and share).

### Featured Placement (Optional Add-On)

- Cost: **$15 AUD / 30 days** (non-recurring; manual re-purchase).
- Label: clearly shown as **“Featured by the platform”**.
- Scope: **council-based only**.
- Allocation: **FIFO**.
- Capacity: **max 5 featured listings per council** at any time.

## 3) Ordering Rules (After Compatibility Filters)

After applying compatibility filters, ordering is:

1. **Featured** listings (FIFO order within the council)
2. **Pro (Gold Card)**
3. **Basic (Free)**

## 4) Refunds and Cancellations

- **No refunds** for Pro or Featured.
- **Pro:** cancel anytime; access remains active until the end of the current 30-day billing period; then stops renewing.
- **Featured:** non-refundable after activation and expires automatically after 30 days.

## 5) Hard Constraints

- Monetisation must **never** affect emergency flows or emergency resource ordering.
- Featured placement is **council-based only** (not suburb-based and not region-based).
- Cap enforcement is strict: **5 per council** maximum, no exceptions.
